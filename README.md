The Bottle Flip Game is a simple yet addictive game where the player attempts to flip a bottle and make it land upright on a flat surface. The game tests timing, accuracy, and control.

In the digital version of the Bottle Flip Game, the player taps or clicks on the screen to apply force to the bottle, causing it to spin in the air. The objective is to land the bottle perfectly upright on platforms or surfaces. Each successful landing earns points, while a failed landing may end the game or reduce lives.

The game can include increasing difficulty levels, moving platforms, obstacles, and score tracking to enhance the challenge and engagement.

🎮 Key Features:
Easy-to-understand gameplay
Score tracking system
Increasing difficulty levels
Physics-based flipping motion
Simple and interactive user interface
🎯 Objective:

To achieve the highest score by successfully flipping and landing the bottle upright multiple times in a row.
